The CompSci Story

Our History

The White Oaks Secondary School Computer Science club have existed for a long time. Our supervisors have extensive experience in teaching and guiding students to improve in the field of compsci and our executives learnt how to teach from previous executives.

Our Goal

Our goal as the WOSS compsci club is to spread awareness of the importance of knowing how to code, as well as teaching the skills of how to be a successive coder to students in White Oaks Secondary School. We see the ability to code as a critical oppertunity for students to become successful in their future.

What do we do

We have meetings every Tuesday in the computer science room in first floor and second floor. In first floor, we teach students applied computer science, which are basic coding that can be usefully applied to real life in useful senarios. In the second floor, we teach competative programming, aimed to prepare students for computer science competitions such as the CCC.

Our Interest

All of our executives and members are extremely passionate about computer science and technology. We are curious in learning, and enjoy problem solving. We try to make computer science fun to learn so that people can enjoy their time in the club while learning usefull knowledge in the field of computer science.

Ours executives

Our executives are very hard working. They prepare lesson plans prior to each meetin in order to efficently deliver the lesson. They have also sacraficed their private time to organize events for the club. Apart from the hard efforts, our executives also have greate achievements on DMOJ and a variety of computer contests.
